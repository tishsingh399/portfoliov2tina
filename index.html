About
The designer
behind the system.

I'm a Senior Product Designer. Most of my work is systems: naming conventions, component governance, where the documentation actually lives versus where people think it does.

A lot of what I do sits between design and engineering. A Figma file has to become real in a codebase, and that translation layer is usually more fragile than anyone planned for. I spend a lot of time in that space.

Currently I'm building a design system across six products at an enterprise security company, working with Angular and React teams. The harder part isn't making things look consistent. It's making sure teams can keep it consistent when I'm not in the room.

I question things that won't hold at scale. I try to do it early.
